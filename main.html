Topics in Distributional Macroeconomics

This website hosts some of the material for the PhD-level course Topics in Distributional Macroeconomics at the Tinbergen Institute Amsterdam. The corresponding Github repository is here.

The first edition of the course was in Spring 2022. Nevertheless, much of the material is unfinished.
